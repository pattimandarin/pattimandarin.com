About me

Patti - Self Intrdoduction

Posted by     "Patti" on Thursday, June 22, 2023

Hi! Everyone, My name is Patti, a native Taiwanese teacher. I majored in Chinese literature and I love reading books and go mountain climbing.

I can teach:

  • How to speak authentic Taiwanese Mandarin (based on living or traveling experiences in Taiwan)

  • Business Chinese in a workplace setting (interviews, communicating with colleagues and clients)

  • Chinese used in popular Taiwanese dramas, movies, and music, with self-made teaching materials

I’m also currently learning Taiwanese and Japanese, I would like to share Taiwanese culture with you. In my classes, I prefer to start with daily life conversations and talk about topics that interest you. I’ll explain how to use grammar in your sentences and encourage you to express yourself comfortably and fluently.

I use self-made teaching materials, and my lesson structure typically starts with daily conversations, followed by teaching correct grammar usage, and ends with discussing articles to improve your reading comprehension. However, if you prefer a different structure, I’m open to using your materials or discussing other class modes.
